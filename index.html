Bio

My research focuses on using LLMs and SLMs on edge for building HRI interfaces for assistive, wearable robots, with an emphasis on personalization of robot actions based on user feedback. I have also worked in using transfer learning for user-based personalization of exoskeleton control based on temporal deep learning methods. Beyond this, I have experience in computer vision, mechatronics, and end-to-end robotic deployment. I am looking for full-time opportunities for ML, AI, robotics or research engineer roles starting May 2026.
