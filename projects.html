Projects

VLM-based Human-Robot Voice Interface

Course: Talking to Robots, Fall 2025

Description: Used supervised finetuning on Qwen2.5-VL with a custom generated dataset of 3000 feedbacks across 3 ambiguity levels and varying scene images for customized parameter control of an exoskeleton based on multimodal inputs.

Automated IV Insertion Bot

Course: Medical Robotics, Fall 2025

Description: Designed and 3D printed linear actuation mechanisms, integrating them with with tiny-YOLO for vision-based vein localization and Arduino based servo motor control for autonomous IV insertion, reducing insertion time by 30% compared to standard human interventions.

Generative Adversarial Imitation Learning (GAIL)

Course: Intro to Robot Learning, Spring 2025

Description: Used GAN based reward models with state and trajectory level approaches for imitating human locomotion from Mocap data.

LQR for Humanoid Balancing

Course: Optimal Control and Reinforcement Learning, Spring 2025

Description: Simulated a humanoid model on a dynamically moving platform in MuJoCo, using an LQR based controller to learn balancing with varying constraint ands conditions.
