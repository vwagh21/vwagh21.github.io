Research

LLM-Guided Adaptive Control for Exoskeletons Using Real-Time User Speech and Scene Context On-device VLMs and LLMs, AI agents, Continual Learning

Closing the loop in wearable robots by using LLMs and VLMs to interpret real-time audio based user feedback and scene context from videos to personalize control for exoskeleton users.

Personalized Torque Prediction for Stroke Populations Transfer Learning, Temporal Convolutional Networks

Used muskuloskeletal simulation software (OpenSim) with MATLAB APIs to generate simulated IMU data from Motion Capture videos of two unaligned datasets.

Trained a temporal convolutional neural network on able-bodied locomotion data, and finetuned it for novel stroke subjects based using transfer learning.

Presented a poster at American Society of Biomechanics meeting 2025 in Pittsburgh highlighting the results of our study.

Evaluating MediaPipe for Post-Stroke Assessment MediaPipe, R, Matlab

Deployed a motion tracking pipeline using OpenCV and MediaPipe pose estimation to analyse upper-limb kinematics (~0.14M actions, ~0.3M frames) & identify key markers of maladaptive compensation strategies in post-stroke individuals..

Evaluating MediaPipe for Fine Upper Limb Motion Capture MediaPipe, R, Matlab, Python

Led a validation study comparing low-cost hand tracking to traditional motion capture using shape similarity analysis (16K fine upper-limb motion trajectories, ~70% accuracy) demonstrating a scalable solution for monitoring fine motor movements in clinical settings.
