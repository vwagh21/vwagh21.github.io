Research @ CMU

Project 1: Gait Prediction with TCNs

Implemented temporal convolutional networks to predict joint torques for lower limb exoskeletons.

Leveraged transfer learning to adapt models from able-bodied to stroke datasets.

Project 2: Walker2D Optimal Control

Developed iLQR-based controllers to teach Walker2D to walk in simulation.

Analyzed stability and robustness across multiple gaits and perturbations.
